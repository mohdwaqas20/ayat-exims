Our Story

Craftsmanship rooted in
over a decade of mastery

At AYAT EXIMS, we are dedicated to producing premium-quality finished leather with a strong foundation in craftsmanship, precision, and innovation. Established in 2009 and based in Kanpur — India's premier leather hub — we have consistently set the benchmark for excellence.

We specialize in finished leather and belt leather, offering a diverse portfolio of over 29 unique prints and textures. Each piece is developed through handmade finishing techniques combined with modern processing, ensuring unmatched durability and refined aesthetics.
